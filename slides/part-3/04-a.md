## Jobs

Job - Highlevel View
<br>
pipeline.yml
```
jobs:
  - name: unit-tests
    plan:
    - get: source-code
    - task: run-tests

  - name: package-publish
    plan:
    - get: source-code
    - task: package
    - put: build-artifact-jar

  - name: deploy-to-dev
    plan:
    - get: source-code
    - get: build-artifact-jar
    - put: deploy-dev-app
```
